About Us

Blossom Together is a student-led humanitarian organization dedicated to improving living standards in developing African countries through initiatives like water wells and medical support, impacting five nations.

To date, we've raised over $200,000, partnered with institutions such as the World Bank, and provided aid to thousands in need through our operations across 10+ US universities.
